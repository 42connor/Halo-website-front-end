What is Halo?

Halo is a groundbreaking read-exclusive brain-machine interface that combines cutting-edge technology with sleek design. Our mission is to create a neural interface that is both fashionable and functional, opening up new possibilities for human-machine interaction.

Halo will be the best in class noninvasive neural interface, generating an entirely new dataset for AI training by placing extremely sensitive electrodes on the user's head to measure brain activity and enable telepathic control of devices.
